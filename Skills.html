Cooking

Cooking is the process of preparing food by applying heat. For example, frying an egg involves cracking the egg into a pan, cooking it until the white is set and the yolk is at your preferred consistency.


Making Crochet

Crochet is a method of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with a hooked needle. For example, making a basic granny square involves creating a series of connected loops and stitches to form a square shape, which can then be used to make blankets, scarves, or other items.

Basic First Aid

Knowing how to handle minor injuries, like applying a bandage to a cut or using an ice pack to reduce swelling.
